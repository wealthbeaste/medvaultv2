📴 Offline Mode
✅ MedVault works fully offline. All your drugs, sales, and orders are saved on this device. When internet comes back, everything syncs automatically.
📱 Install as App
On Android (Chrome):
1. Open MedVault in Chrome
2. Tap the 3-dot menu (⋮)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Add"
✅ Opens like a real app!
On iPhone (Safari):
1. Open MedVault in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (□↑)
3. Scroll to "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
👤 Staff Accounts
Manage who can log in to your pharmacy
How staff log in:
1. You create an account for them using the Invite Staff button above
2. You give them their email and password
3. They go to your Netlify site → app.html and log in with those credentials
4. They will only see their assigned branch — not other branches

🏪 Branches
Manage all locations in your organisation
Multi-branch works like this:
Each branch has its own inventory, sales, and staff. As the owner you can see all branches from this dashboard. Staff assigned to a branch only see that branch when they log in. You can transfer stock between branches from the Stock Transfers section below.
