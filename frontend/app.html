📴 Offline Mode
✅ MedVault works fully offline. All your drugs, sales, and orders are saved on this device. When internet comes back, everything syncs automatically.
📱 Install as App
On Android (Chrome):
1. Open MedVault in Chrome
2. Tap the 3-dot menu (⋮)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Add"
✅ Opens like a real app!
On iPhone (Safari):
1. Open MedVault in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (□↑)
3. Scroll to "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
